🍝 Creamy White Sauce Pasta

Easy • Delicious • Homemade

About the Recipe

This creamy white sauce pasta is rich, cheesy, and packed with delicious flavors. It is perfect for lunch, dinner, or whenever you're craving a comforting homemade meal.

Ingredients

  • 250 g Pasta
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 2 tbsp Maida
  • 2 cups Milk
  • 3 Cheese Cubes
  • 1 tbsp White Mayonnaise
  • 2 Medium Onions (finely chopped)
  • 2–3 Garlic Cloves (finely chopped)
  • 1 Packet Maggi Masala
  • 1 tbsp Schezwan Sauce
  • Oregano
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Fresh Cream / Malai (Optional)

Preparation Steps

  1. Boil the pasta until it is soft.
  2. Heat oil and butter in a pan.
  3. Add chopped garlic and sauté for about a minute.
  4. Add chopped onions and cook until translucent.
  5. Add maida and cook for 2 minutes on low flame.
  6. Slowly pour in the milk while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  7. Add black pepper and cook until the sauce comes to a gentle boil.
  8. Add Maggi Masala, Schezwan Sauce and White Mayonnaise.
  9. Add cheese cubes and stir until completely melted.
  10. Add the boiled pasta and mix well.
  11. Garnish with oregano and chilli flakes. Serve hot.

Recipe Information

Preparation Time 15 Minutes
Cooking Time 20 Minutes
Servings 4 People
Difficulty Easy

👨‍🍳 Chef's Tips

  • Add some oil in the boiling water to prevent the pasta from sticking.
  • Add fresh cream or malai in the end for extra richness.
  • Serve immediately for the creamiest and most delicious pasta.
